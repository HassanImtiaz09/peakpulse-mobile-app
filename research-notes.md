# PeakPulse AI - Pre-Launch Research Notes

## App Store Requirements
- Apple: $99/year developer account, 15-30% commission, 24-72h review, very strict
- Google: $25 one-time, 15-30% commission, hours to 7 days review, moderately strict
- Both require: privacy policy, accurate metadata, no crashes, demo account for reviewers
- iOS: Must use Apple IAP for digital goods, must support account deletion
- Android: AAB format required, target API 34+, content rating questionnaire
- 88% rejections due to: crashes, misleading metadata, privacy violations, IAP issues

## Key Pre-Submission Assets Needed
- App icon (1024x1024 for iOS, 512x512 for Play Store)
- Screenshots: iPhone 6.7" and 6.1" (iOS), phone + 7" + 10" tablet (Android)
- App preview video (optional but recommended)
- Privacy policy URL
- Support URL
- Marketing URL (landing page)
- Demo account credentials for reviewers

## Health/Fitness App Specific
- Privacy policy must cover health data handling
- Apple Health/HealthKit integration requires specific privacy disclosures
- FTC requires clear disclosure of data collection practices
- AI features must be disclosed and get user consent (Apple 2025 policy)

## Monetization Strategy (Fitness Apps)
- Freemium + subscription is dominant model
- 15% commission on subscriptions (Apple Small Business Program)
- Free tier with basic features, premium for AI features, meal plans, wearable sync
- Average fitness app subscription: $9.99-$14.99/month or $59.99-$99.99/year

## Marketing Strategy
- Pre-launch: landing page, social media presence, email waitlist
- Launch: ASO (App Store Optimization), influencer partnerships, PR
- Post-launch: content marketing, referral program, paid acquisition
- Fitness app market: $12.1B in 2025, growing to $45.45B by 2035 (14% CAGR)
